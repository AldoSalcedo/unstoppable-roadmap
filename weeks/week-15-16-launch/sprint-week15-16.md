#### SALARY NEGOTIATION SCRIPTS

**SCENARIO 1: First Offer Received**

**Interviewer says**: "We'd like to offer you the role. The base salary is $75k."

**Your response** (DO NOT accept immediately):

```
[English]
"Thank you so much for the offer! I'm very excited about the opportunity.
Before I give you an answer, can we discuss the complete compensation package?

I was looking at the range of $85-95k based on:
- My experience with [relevant tech] and healthcare domain knowledge
- Market research on similar roles in [city/remote market]
- The scope of the role (I noticed it includes [responsibility])

What flexibility do you have on the base, and can we discuss the full package
including bonus, equity, and benefits?"

[Spanish]
"¡Muchas gracias por la oferta! Estoy muy emocionado sobre la oportunidad.

Antes de que responda, ¿podemos discutir el paquete de compensación completo?

Estaba buscando en el rango de $85-95k basado en:
- Mi experiencia con [tech relevante] y conocimiento del sector healthcare
- Investigación de mercado en roles similares en [ciudad/mercado remoto]
- El alcance del rol (noté que incluye [responsabilidad])

¿Qué flexibilidad tienes en la base, y podemos discutir el paquete completo
incluyendo bonus, equity, y beneficios?"
```

**Key principles**:
- ✅ Never accept first offer
- ✅ Show research (market data)
- ✅ Anchor high ($85-95k if they said $75k)
- ✅ Ask about full package (bonus, equity, benefits)
- ✅ Use "I was looking at..." (softer than "I want...")

---

**SCENARIO 2: Negotiation Back-and-Forth**

**Interviewer**: "We can offer $80k, but that's our top on salary. We're limited on base."

**Your response**:

```
[English]
"I appreciate that flexibility. $80k is closer to my range. Since we're bumping up
against the salary ceiling, can we explore other components?

A few questions:
1. What's the bonus structure? (I saw 15-20% is typical for similar roles)
2. Can we discuss equity/options? How much, vesting schedule, strike price?
3. Any sign-on bonus (relocation, onboarding)?
4. Professional development budget?
5. Remote flexibility? (This is important for my work-life balance)

If we get creative with bonus + equity + benefits, the total comp could land
in my $90-100k range even if base is $80k."

[Spanish]
"Aprecio esa flexibilidad. $80k está más cerca de mi rango. Ya que estamos
en el límite del salario, ¿podemos explorar otros componentes?

Algunas preguntas:
1. ¿Cuál es la estructura de bonus? (Vi que 15-20% es típico para roles similares)
2. ¿Podemos discutir equity/opciones? ¿Cuánto, cronograma de vesting?
3. ¿Hay bonus de firma o reubicación?
4. ¿Presupuesto de desarrollo profesional?
5. ¿Flexibilidad remota? (Esto es importante para mi balance)

Si somos creativos con bonus + equity + beneficios, la compensación total
podría estar en mi rango de $90-100k incluso si la base es $80k."
```

---

**SCENARIO 3: Two Offers Competing**

If you get 2+ offers, use leverage:

```
[English]
"I'm excited about both opportunities. Company A has offered $85k + 0.5% equity.
Company B offered $80k + 1% equity + 20% bonus.

Before I decide, I wanted to see if you can match or exceed [offer term].
I'm genuinely interested in [Company], and I'd love to make it work.

Can you offer:
- $87k base + 0.75% equity, or
- $85k + 1.5% equity + 15% bonus?

I want to move fast — I can commit by [date] if we can close on terms."

[Spanish]
"Estoy emocionado con ambas oportunidades. Empresa A ofreció $85k + 0.5% equity.
Empresa B ofreció $80k + 1% equity + 20% bonus.

Antes de decidir, quería ver si puedes igualar o superar [offer term].
Estoy genuinamente interesado en [Empresa], y me gustaría que funcione.

¿Puedes ofrecer:
- $87k base + 0.75% equity, o
- $85k + 1.5% equity + 15% bonus?

Quiero moverme rápido — puedo comprometerme por [date] si cerramos términos."
```

---

**KEY NEGOTIATION PRINCIPLES**:

1. **Never say the first number** — Let them anchor low, then counter-offer high.
2. **Always get data** — Use levels.fyi, glassdoor, blind to back up your range.
3. **Show enthusiasm** — "I'm excited about this, I want to make it work..."
4. **Be creative** — If salary is fixed, negotiate bonus, equity, benefits, remote, PD budget.
5. **Use leverage** — "I have another offer..." (if true) is powerful.
6. **Know your BATNA** — Minimum acceptable offer (your walk-away number).
7. **Move fast** — Compress timeline once you're close ("I can decide by Friday").
