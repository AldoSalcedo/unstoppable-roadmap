## IF YOU GET STUCK...

### "I'm not getting responses"
- **Week 15**: This is normal. Applications take 3-5 days to screen.
- **Action**: Increase volume (submit 20+ more applications). Customize better. Find hiring manager emails for cold outreach.
- **Parallel**: Go heavy on freelance (immediate income potential).

### "Interviews are going badly"
- **Problem**: You're probably not practicing enough or not listening well.
- **Action**: Do 10 LeetCode problems minimum per day. Record yourself answering questions. Identify weaknesses (technical, communication, behavioral). Fix.
- **Parallel**: Freelance proposals (lower stakes, build confidence).

### "No freelance leads"
- **Problem**: Your outreach isn't resonating or you're not targeting right people.
- **Action**: Research more companies. Find actual CTOs / Founders on LinkedIn. Write personalized pitches (not templates). Increase Upwork proposals (100+ if needed). Use Toptal pre-screened tier (credibility).

### "Morale is low"
- **Problem**: Job search is brutal. You're putting yourself out there, facing rejection.
- **Action**: Celebrate small wins (every screen, every response is a win). Call a friend. Remember: 100 applications → 30 screens → 10 technical → 2-3 offers. You're in a numbers game. Keep playing.
- **Parallel**: Freelance wins (real income, builds confidence).

### "I'm tired"
- **Problem**: 14 days of intensity is exhausting.
- **Action**: Take a 2-hour break today. Recharge. But finish the day with 5 applications + 10 LinkedIn outreaches. Momentum is everything.
