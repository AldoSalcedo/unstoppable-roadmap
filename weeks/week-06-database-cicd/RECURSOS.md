## Exam Prep Timeline (Week 6)

### Monday-Wednesday (Days 1-3)
- Watch 2-3 hours of AWS course content (focus on RDS, IAM, Secrets Manager)
- Take 1 practice exam (identify weak areas)

### Thursday (Day 4)
- Review weak areas (typically: IAM policies, DynamoDB, Lambda)
- Watch targeted videos on weak topics

### Friday (Day 5)
- Take 2 full practice exams (3 hours each)
- Review missed questions (analyze why you missed each)

### Saturday (Day 6)
- Final review: RDS, IAM, CodePipeline, CloudWatch
- Get plenty of sleep (NOT last-minute cramming)

### Sunday (Day 7)
- Exam day!
- Breakfast + hydration + arrive early
- Trust your preparation
