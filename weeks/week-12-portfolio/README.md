# Semana 12: Portfolio & Professional Branding Week

## Overview

This is the final week of the 12-week roadmap—your portfolio sprint. You're not just building a website; you're crafting your **professional narrative** as a Healthcare Software Engineer transitioning from biology/QBP and auditing into Senior Software Engineering.

This week focuses on:
- **Portfolio Website**: Modern, professional Next.js site showcasing your unique healthcare domain expertise
- **Open Source Contributions**: Demonstrate real-world impact through OSS participation
- **Professional Branding**: LinkedIn, resume, personal blog optimized for discovery
- **SEO & Analytics**: Make sure decision-makers can find you

## Why This Matters

You have a **unique positioning**:
- Biology/QBP background → Deep domain knowledge in healthcare
- Auditing experience → Attention to compliance, security, data integrity
- AWS/Azure certifications → Cloud-native, enterprise-ready skills
- Multilingual (Spanish/English) → Can work with global healthcare teams

Your portfolio is the proof. It's where you show, don't tell.
