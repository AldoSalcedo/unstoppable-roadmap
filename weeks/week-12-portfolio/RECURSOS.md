## LinkedIn Optimization

### LinkedIn Content Strategy

- **LinkedIn's Official Blog**: https://blog.linkedin.com
- **LinkedIn Learning - Personal Branding**: https://www.linkedin.com/learning/personal-branding
- **LinkedIn Creator Hub**: https://business.linkedin.com/marketing-solutions/content-marketing

### LinkedIn Profile Optimization

- **LinkedIn Profile Keywords**: Check "Skills" section recommendations
- **LinkedIn Headline Tips**: 120 character limit; use keywords
- **LinkedIn About Section**: https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/352

### LinkedIn Content Types

**Thought Leadership Posts**:
- Share insights from your work
- Discuss industry trends
- Ask questions to engage
- Post 1-2x per week

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Buffer**: https://buffer.com (schedule posts)
- **Later**: https://later.com (content planning)
- **Adobe Express**: https://www.adobe.com/express/ (create LinkedIn visuals)

### LinkedIn Engagement

- **LinkedIn Articles**: Write long-form content (10+ min reads)
- **LinkedIn Posts**: Share daily insights, wins, learnings
- **LinkedIn Videos**: Short clips (under 2 min) perform best

### LinkedIn Recommendations

- Ask colleagues/managers for recommendations
- Write recommendations first (reciprocity)
- Specific, quantified recommendations are most valuable

### Blogging Platforms

- **Dev.to**: https://dev.to (free, built-in audience)
- **Medium**: https://medium.com (paywall + audience)
- **Substack**: https://substack.com (newsletter format)

### Writing Resources

- **Google's Technical Writing Course**: https://developers.google.com/tech-writing
- **Hemingway Editor**: https://hemingwayapp.com (writing clarity)
- **Grammarly**: https://www.grammarly.com (grammar + style)

### Blog Post Structure

**Typical Tech Blog Post**:
1. Hook (why should I read this?)
2. Context (what problem are we solving?)
3. Solution (how do we solve it?)
4. Code examples (show, don't tell)
5. Results/Lessons (what did we learn?)
6. Conclusion + call to action

### SEO for Blog Posts

- **Yoast SEO Guide**: https://yoast.com/seo/ (free guide)
- **Moz Keyword Research**: https://moz.com/keyword-research
- **Google Search Console**: Track which posts rank for what keywords
