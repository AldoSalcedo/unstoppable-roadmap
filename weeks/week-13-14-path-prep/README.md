## Overview

After 12 weeks of foundational work, you're ready to choose your path. Weeks 13-14 are an **intensive preparation block** for one of three directions:

1. **Path 1: Specialist** - Senior Healthcare Software Engineer ($100-150/hr consulting)
2. **Path 2: Leader** - Engineering Team Lead → VP Engineering trajectory
3. **Path 3: Builder** - Founder / SaaS Builder with sustainable unit economics

This block combines **intensive interview prep, system design mastery, strategic networking, and path-specific validation**.

## What You'll Do

### Week 13: Path Selection & Interview Excellence
- **Path Selection:** Deep dive into your three options (research companies, talk to mentors, analyze financial projections)
- **Mock Interviews:** 5+ sessions covering behavioral + technical rounds
- **System Design:** Healthcare EMR system deep dive (aligns with your domain knowledge)
- **Networking:** Strategic LinkedIn outreach for meaningful connections

### Week 14: Path-Specific Execution
- **Path 1 (Specialist):** HIPAA certification prep, healthcare tech landscape analysis, consulting rate positioning
- **Path 2 (Leader):** Engineering management fundamentals, team dynamics, Spotify/Basecamp model studies
- **Path 3 (Builder):** SaaS idea validation, user interview template, MVP scoping for your idea

### Common Thread: Path Commitment
By Day 14, commit to your chosen path with a clear 90-day plan.
