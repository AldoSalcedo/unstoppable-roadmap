**DÍA 9: Consulting Rate & Positioning (5 horas)**

- [ ] Research consulting rates:
  - Study: Toptal, Gun.io, Expert Exchange (healthcare engineer rates)
  - Calculate your rate:
    ```
    Hourly Rate = (Desired Annual Income / 1000 billable hours) + margin

    Example: Want $200k/year
    = ($200,000 / 1000) + 20% markup
    = $200 + $40 = $240/hr (but market may support $100-150/hr)

    Your calculation:
    - Desired income: $___/year
    - Billable hours: 1000/year (20 hrs/week, 50 weeks)
    - Rate: $___/hr
    ```

- [ ] Build positioning statement:
  ```
  "I'm a senior software engineer specialized in healthcare systems,
  with expertise in HIPAA compliance, React/React Native frontend,
  and AWS/Azure cloud architecture. I've built [project examples]
  and hold [certifications]. Available for consulting at $[rate]/hr
  on [type of engagements]."
  ```

- [ ] Action items:
  - [ ] Update LinkedIn headline: "Senior Healthcare Engineer | React | AWS/Azure | HIPAA-Compliant Systems"
  - [ ] Create 1-page consulting overview (what you offer, rates, process)
  - [ ] Reach out to 5 healthcare companies: "Open to advisory/consulting"

**DÍA 9: User Interviews + MVP Scoping (5 horas)**

- [ ] User interview template (prepare 30 min):
  ```
  "Hi [Name], I'm exploring an idea to [problem statement].
  I'd love to understand your workflow better.

  - Can you walk me through how you currently [solve this problem]?
  - What's painful about your current approach?
  - What would ideal look like?
  - Would you pay for a solution? (If so, how much?)
  - Who else faces this problem?"
  ```

- [ ] Conduct 5 user interviews (3 hours):
  - Interview people in your target market
  - Take notes on:
    - Their main pain point (in their words)
    - Current solution (what they do today)
    - Willingness to pay
    - Key features they'd need

- [ ] MVP Scope (1.5 horas):
  - Define: Minimum viable set of features
  - Build: 2-week technical roadmap
  - Estimate: Time to MVP (engineering hours)
  - Document: "80/20 Rule" (which 20% of features solve 80% of the problem?)

  - [ ] Update LinkedIn: "Engineer exploring healthcare SaaS | Founder in waiting | Building X"
